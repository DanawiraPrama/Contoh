Strategic Timeline & Roadmap

STEP 1: RESTRUCTURED
Broad Campaigns Deactivation

Lucerne (Broad) was fully deactivated on July 6 due to conversion fatigue and high overall CPL. This successfully cut waste on high-frequency, non-converting cold broad segments.

STEP 2: RUNNING (LLA 6%)
Restructuring Into LLA 6%

July 6 launched new high-intent Lookalike Audiences (LLA 6%) for Thomson and Lucerne. Currently, they show strong click signals (Thomson LLA CTR at 1.03%) but are suffering from high CPM thresholds. Let the algorithm optimize.

STEP 3: ACTION ITEMS
Scale Success, Trim Fatigue

Reallocate budget from Thomson Broad (CPL SGD 71.18) to Hougang (CPL SGD 20.51). Provide creative freshness to Lucerne's LLA relaunch to bypass high CPMs.
